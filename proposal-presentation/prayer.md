# Opening Prayer — Proposal Defense

Read aloud after the chair introduces the panel and the proponents introduce
themselves, just before the opening hook. Keep it short (~20–30 seconds).

---

## Prayer

Let us begin with a prayer.

Heavenly Father,

We thank You for the gift of this day and for the knowledge, guidance, and
opportunity that brought us to this moment. As we present our work, grant us
clear minds, calm hearts, and honest words.

Bless our panel with wisdom and patience as they listen and guide us, and help
us to receive their insights with humility and gratitude.

May whatever we do here serve a good purpose and give honor to You.

This we ask in Your holy name.

Amen.

---

## Shorter alternative (~15 seconds)

Heavenly Father, we thank You for this day and for the wisdom You have given us.
Guide our words, steady our hearts, and bless our panel with patience and insight.
May our work honor You. Amen.
